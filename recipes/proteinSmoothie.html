Protein Smoothie

Description

This recipe packs a punch in how many calories and protein it contains. It has various components that are blended to provide a quick nutrient filled meal

  • Two Scoops of Chocolate Protein
  • One Egg
  • One Banana
  • Three Cups of Whole Milk
  • Half-Cup of Oats
  1. Pour milk into a blender.
  2. Add all other ingredients.
  3. Blend to desired consistency.
