Chicken Quesadilla

Description

This simple recipe delivers a tasty and protein filled meal.
The quesadilla is made by using two low calorie flour tortillas along with chicken and colby jack cheese.

  • Two Low Calorie Flour Tortillas
  • Thin Sliced Chicken Breast
  • Three Slices of Colby Jack Cheese
  1. Using pan, cook the chicken breast with desired seasoning if wanted.
  2. Once cooked, cut breast into small pieces.
  3. Heat two tortillas on a skillet and apply one slice of cheese to each.
  4. Add chicken along with another slice on top of one of the tortillas.
  5. Combine both tortillas and flip until cheese is melted.
