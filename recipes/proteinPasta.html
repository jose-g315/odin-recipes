Protein Pasta

Description

This recipe involves packing protein into a pasta dish. Instead of regular pasta this recipe uses chickpea pasta along with ground turkey.
Any type of sauce can be used but the Rao's pasta sauce pairs well with it.

  • ChickPea Pasta (8oz)
  • Ground Turkey (1lb)
  • Rao's Pasta Sauce (24oz)
  1. Boil 6 cups of water.
  2. Add entire box of pasta to water.
  3. Cook to desired tenderness
  4. Brown ground turkey in a pan
  5. Drain excess fat.
  6. Add pasta sauce and bring to a boil.
  7. Combine pasta and sauce to enjoy!
